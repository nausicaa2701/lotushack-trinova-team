Market Challenge

Problem

For EV Owners

  • Hard to find EV-safe car washes
  • No on-route discovery
  • Lack of trust in providers

For Providers

  • Limited visibility to EV customers
  • No demand forecasting
  • Difficulty standing out

For Operators

  • No ecosystem oversight
  • Manual quality control
  • Limited growth insights

Our Solution

WashNet Platform

🔍 Smart Discovery

Nearby and on-route search with EV-safe filtering, ranking, and real-time availability.

📅 Seamless Booking

One-click booking with time slot recommendations and confirmation tracking.

🤖 AI-Powered

Demand forecasting, ranking models, and slot recommendations powered by machine learning.

📊 Operator Dashboard

Ecosystem oversight, quality signals, campaign management, and dispute resolution.
